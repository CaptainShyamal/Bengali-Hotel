About Bengali Hotel

Bengali Hotel was born from a simple desire: to serve homely, nostalgic Bengali food with the finesse of a boutique restaurant.

From the bustling streets of Kolkata to the serene riverbanks of the Ganges, each dish on our menu tells a story of heritage, warmth, and celebration. We carefully source our ingredients and grind our spices in‑house to keep every plate honest and flavorful.
